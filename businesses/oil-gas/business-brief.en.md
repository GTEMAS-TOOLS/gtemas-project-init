# Business Brief - Oil Gas

## Industry Overview
Oil and gas companies operate across upstream (exploration, drilling, production), midstream (transportation, storage), and downstream (refining, distribution, retail). Operations are capital-intensive with complex supply chains and strict safety/environmental requirements.

Digital transformation focuses on IoT-driven production optimization, predictive maintenance for aging infrastructure, digital twin technology, and ESG reporting.

Core challenges include volatile commodity prices, HSE compliance, aging workforce knowledge transfer, remote asset management, and increasing ESG/sustainability mandates.

## Key Pain Points
- Volatile commodity prices requiring agile cost management
- Aging infrastructure and high maintenance costs
- Stringent HSE requirements (fatalities, spills, emissions)
- Remote asset locations with connectivity challenges
- Knowledge loss from retiring experienced workforce
- Increasing ESG and decarbonization pressure from investors

## Target Personas
- **Production Manager**: Well optimization, production targets, cost control
- **HSE Manager**: Safety incidents, environmental compliance, risk assessment
- **Drilling Engineer**: Well planning, drilling optimization, cost per foot
- **Supply Chain Manager**: Procurement, logistics, inventory at remote sites
- **Reservoir Engineer**: Reserve estimation, production forecasting, enhanced recovery

## Key Performance Indicators
- Production uptime (target: >95%)
- HSE Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR <0.5)
- Lifting cost per barrel
- Reserve replacement ratio
- Flaring reduction percentage
- Equipment availability

## Regulatory & Compliance Landscape
- EPA/environmental agency regulations
- OSHA workplace safety standards
- Pipeline safety regulations (PHMSA)
- SEC reserve reporting requirements
- Greenhouse gas emissions reporting

## Typical Digital Maturity
Oil & gas has adopted SCADA, historians, and production management but digital integration between subsurface, surface, and business systems remains fragmented. Offshore and remote operations face connectivity constraints.
