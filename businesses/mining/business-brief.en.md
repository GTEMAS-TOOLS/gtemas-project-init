# Business Brief - Mining

## Industry Overview
Mining operations encompass exploration, extraction, processing, and reclamation across metals, minerals, and coal. Operations are capital-intensive with long project lifecycles and strict environmental and safety obligations.

Key challenges include remote site operations, equipment utilization, worker safety in hazardous environments, environmental compliance, community relations, and commodity price volatility.

Digital transformation focuses on autonomous haulage systems, IoT-enabled equipment health monitoring, digital mine planning, real-time grade control, and ESG reporting to meet sustainability mandates.

## Key Pain Points
- Equipment downtime costing $100K+/hour for large haul trucks
- Safety incidents in hazardous underground and open-pit environments
- Environmental compliance and mine closure financial obligations
- Ore grade variability impacting processing efficiency and recovery
- Remote site workforce management, fatigue, and retention
- Community and indigenous stakeholder relations management

## Target Personas
- **Mine Manager**: Production targets, safety culture, cost control, regulatory compliance
- **Geologist / Resource Engineer**: Resource modeling, grade control, reserve estimation
- **Equipment / Fleet Manager**: Fleet utilization, maintenance strategy, equipment availability
- **HSE Manager**: Incident prevention, emergency response, environmental compliance
- **Processing Plant Manager**: Throughput, recovery rates, reagent efficiency, quality

## Key Performance Indicators
- Equipment availability (target: >90%)
- Safety Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR < 1.0)
- Cost per tonne mined
- Ore recovery rate and head grade
- Environmental incident count
- Mine plan compliance percentage

## Regulatory & Compliance Landscape
- Mine safety regulations (MSHA / equivalent)
- Environmental impact assessment and permit requirements
- Tailings dam safety regulations (MAC/ICOLD guidelines)
- Indigenous land rights consultation requirements
- Mineral royalty and resource taxation regulations

## Typical Digital Maturity
Mining has adopted fleet management and process historian systems but integration between geological models, mine planning, and real-time operations remains fragmented. Processing plants have DCS but limited predictive analytics. Autonomous haulage is emerging in large open-pit mines.
