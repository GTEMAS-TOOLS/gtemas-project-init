# Business Brief - Automotive

## Industry Overview
Automotive companies design, manufacture, and sell vehicles through dealer networks. Operations span product lifecycle management, supply chain, manufacturing, quality, and after-sales service.

## Key Pain Points
- Manual processes reducing operational efficiency
- Data silos across departments
- Regulatory compliance burden
- Difficulty retaining specialized talent
- Limited real-time visibility into operations

## Target Personas
- **CEO/Director**: Strategy, growth, compliance
- **Operations Manager**: Efficiency, quality, delivery
- **IT Manager**: Systems, integration, security
- **Finance Controller**: Budgets, reporting, audit

## Key Performance Indicators
- Operational efficiency improvement
- Cost reduction percentage
- Customer/stakeholder satisfaction
- Compliance audit score
- Digital adoption rate

## Regulatory & Compliance Landscape
- Industry-specific regulations
- Data protection laws
- Financial reporting standards
- Labor regulations
- Environmental standards

## Typical Digital Maturity
Organizations in this sector have adopted basic operational systems but face integration challenges. Digital transformation opportunities exist in automation, analytics, and customer/stakeholder engagement.
